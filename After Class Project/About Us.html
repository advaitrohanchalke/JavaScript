Food

Food is any substance, typically composed of carbohydrates, fats, proteins, vitamins, and minerals, that living organisms consume to sustain life, provide energy, and promote growth. Derived primarily from plants, animals, or fungi, it undergoes digestion and assimilation within the body to maintain vital cellular functions, repair tissues, and support the immune system. Beyond its biological necessity, food plays a critical role in human culture, economy, and social bonding, with its preparation and consumption varying widely across different global traditions.

Chinese Cuisines

Veg Fried Rice

This dish is Veg Fried Rice, an immensely popular Indo-Chinese street food and restaurant favorite. Depending on the specific hot sauces or spice blends folded into it during the stir-fry process, it is often prepared as Veg Schezwan Fried Rice.

Veg Hakka Noodles

This dish is Veg Hakka Noodles, also popularly referred to as Veg Chow Mein. It is one of the most celebrated and commonly ordered staple dishes of Indo-Chinese cuisine.
